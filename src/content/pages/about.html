About Us

Cactus Web Designs

Making Your Small Business Compete

My name is Jake, I am the owner and sole developer at Cactus Web Designs. As a former collegiate athlete, I know what it takes to compete. I know what it takes to put countless hours of hard work into building something you are proud of. Over the course of more than 5 years I have perfected what it takes to hand code websites the right way.

I realized many small businesses can't afford to shell out thousands of dollars at once for a new website. Plus, the ones who do often end up regretting it because they get stuck with awful, outdated websites. It's a tough spot to be in – either you can't afford a decent website, or you don't know who to trust to actually deliver a good one. So, I changed up how I do business to help them out. That led me to offer a plan where businesses pay nothing upfront and just $150 a month. It's a way for small businesses to get a website they need without breaking the bank.
