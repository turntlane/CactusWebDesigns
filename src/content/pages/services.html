Services

OUR PROCESS

1. Review Site

Before we get started, we want to know if you have a site, is it currently organized, and whether it’s in-line with the best practices for your industry. If not, all the better, we start from scratch. We’ll make suggestions for how to re-organize your pages and navigation, visually delivered via wireframe.

2. Collect Website Assets

Maintaining brand consistency is important in a website, as visitors should recognize immediately that it's your brand. We will collect assets to properly represent your brand.

3. Create Design

We will create a design for you and go over it together. You will point out changes you’d like to see and the parts we got just right so it is tailored for you and your brand.

4. Conduct Quality Assurance Testing

Once you give us the thumbs-up on your custom web design, we’ll make sure the look and layout remain consistent across all devices (desktop, mobile and tablet) operating systems (including iOS and Android) and browsers, (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari and others).

Design

Web Design & Development Done Differently

What makes us unique in the development world is that we don't use WordPress, frameworks, or page builders. We meticulously write the code line by line. This gives us much more control over the design, and the website performs lightning fast because there's no bloated or messy code that slows it down.

Websites built by hand will out-perform most WordPress sites and drag and drop page builders which could cost thousands of dollars. When it comes to the web, the faster and more organized websites win.

SEO

Search Engine Optimization

SEO is really important for small business websites because it helps them show up higher on search engine results, which means more people can find them easily. It's a cost-effective way to get noticed online, especially when competing against bigger companies. With the right keywords and a website that's easy to use, small businesses can draw in more visitors and turn them into customers, helping their business grow.
